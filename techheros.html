Dr. Iretioluwa Akerele

/>

Dr. Akerele is a leader in the cybersecurity field and co-founder of CyBlack, a nonprofit that provides resources and free education to those seeking cybersecurity training in order to remove obstacles and increase diversity in the field. She is also involved in initiatives that aim to encourage and help young African women to pursue careers in cybersecurity.

Kimberly Bryant

Kimberly Bryant is an electrical engineer who founded Black Girls Code, which focuses on exposing young, underrepresented girls to coding and computers. The bulk of her career has been spent as a Biotechnology Engineer at companies such as Genentech and Pfizer. She is a champion of breaking barriers, fighting for equity, and prioritizing societal advancement by giving back to her community.
