Dr Iretioluwa Akerele

Dr. Akerele is a leader in the cybersecurity field and co-founder of CyBlack, a nonprofit that provides resources and free education to those seeking cybersecurity training in order to remove obstacles and increase diversity in the field. She is also involved in initiatives that aim to encourage and help young African women to pursue careers in cybersecurity.
