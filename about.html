About Maggie

Hi everyone! My name is Maggie and I am a senior studying electrical engineering and computer science at UW-Madison. In my free time, I sing in the choir at St. Paul's, do crafts such as drawing and crocheting, read, and try different hobbies. I took this class to learn more about website development and because I was interested in the intersection of concepts I learned in sociology with topics more related to my majors. My favorite 'tech' thing currently is my biomedical instrumentation course because we apply many of the electrical engineering concepts I have learned in various courses to medical technology and get to build medical devices.
